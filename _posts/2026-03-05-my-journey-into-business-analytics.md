Hello! I'm **Anurag Elluru**, a Business Analyst with over four years of experience working with data-driven systems and operational workflows.

My journey into analytics began during my time at **Dunzo**, a quick-commerce startup backed by Google. While working with operations and technical teams, I saw how data could improve delivery performance, reduce delays, and help teams make better decisions.

This experience motivated me to deepen my analytical skills, which led me to pursue a **Master’s in Business Analytics at the University of Central Florida**.

During my graduate program, I worked on several data projects including:

• Modeling driver cancellation behavior using logistic regression and Random Forest models  
• Forecasting demand patterns using transaction data  
• Building dashboards to analyze operational performance  

Through these experiences, I developed skills in:

- SQL  
- Python  
- Power BI and Tableau  
- Data workflow validation and testing  
- Business process analysis  

This website is where I will share my projects, research, and thoughts on analytics, data systems, and operational optimization.

Thank you for visiting!
